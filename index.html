Memory Card Game Welcome

One Player

The game is played solo and implements a level system.
As you progress through levels, the game becomes more challenging.
Within the specified seconds, you must find pairs of all cards!
If you can't find pairs for all cards within the given time, the game restarts from the beginning.

Two Player

In the two-player mode, there is no time limit. The player who finds the most pairs of cards wins!
